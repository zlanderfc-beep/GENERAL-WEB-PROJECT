About Great Computer Engineers

Grace Hopper

Pioneer of computer programming, developed the first compiler and contributed to COBOL language.

Alan Turing

Father of theoretical computer science and artificial intelligence, known for breaking the Enigma code.

Ada Lovelace

Regarded as the first computer programmer, wrote the first algorithm for Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine.

Tim Berners-Lee

Inventor of the World Wide Web, revolutionized information sharing and accessibility globally.

Dennis Ritchie

Creator of the C programming language and co-developer of UNIX, foundational to modern computing.
